About Me

My name is Christopher Durham, though I usually go by my screen name, CAD97, on the internet. I'm a college student at Furman University, studying for a dual major with a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Music. I like to create things, and this is a central repository where these things end up. I do everything from single-webpage mini-applets to a decently-sized game built with Unity, and a few musical endeavors have made it onto this site as well.
